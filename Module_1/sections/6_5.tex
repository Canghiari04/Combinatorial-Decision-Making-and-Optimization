One thing is about learning and an other thing is about the variable choice. So far, we chose variables randomly, but also in the SAT problems the variable
choice is very important. \vspace{7pt}

The choice of the variable is already integrated by the solvers, based on the concept of \textbf{activity-based search}. They concentrate on the variables 
causing conflicts, or, in other words, they focus on those variables that appear different times in conflict clauses and in new derived clauses. Each time a variable 
is involved in these kind of clauses solvers increment its weight associated to it. Therefore, we should select the boolean variable with \textbf{highest weight}.
