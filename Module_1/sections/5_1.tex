Why do we care about SAT? SAT is the first proven \textbf{NP-complete} decision problem (\textit{NP is the complexity class between the tractable and 
intractable problems}). \vspace{7pt}

All problems in the complexity class NP, including a wide range of decision and optimization problems, are reducible to SAT in polynomial time: if we can 
reduce those problems in polynomial time to a SAT problem, we can prove that those problems are at most difficult to solve as SAT. Solving a SAT problem 
we could determine that a problem is NP-complete.
