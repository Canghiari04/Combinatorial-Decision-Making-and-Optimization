After the second chapter, we are able to design a model composed by decision variables and constraints. In addition, we stated that the choices done during 
the modelling phase could affect the size of the search space and the performance of constraint propagation algorithm. Anyway, 
what is a \textbf{constraint propagation algorithm}?
\begin{definition}[title = Constraint propagation]
    Constraint propagation is a process used to examine every constraint to \textbf{remove inconsistent values} from the domains of the \textbf{future decision variables}.
\end{definition}
